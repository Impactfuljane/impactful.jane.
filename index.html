Core Values at the heart of Embrace Your Scars

I have been on a purpose-driven path to Re-writing the trajectory of my Life's story with Partnership of the Holy Spirit!

1.

Advocating and educating victims, pointing them to resources that'll enable them to get on the road to genuine healing, forgiveness and emotional prosperity according to 3 John 1:2.

2.

Uncovering the lid, and exposing the despicable evil known as domestic violence in our culture and the body of Christ at large.

3.

Creating awareness by educating and lending my voice to change the societal conditioning that victims remain and die in abusive relationships.

4.

Normalizing the concept of getting help as an embodiment of strength and changing the narrative that getting help is weakness.

5.

Teaching the message of redemption and reconciliation through Christ, while uncovering scriptures that support the notion that God isn't pleased with the concept of suffering and smiling in abusive relationships.

6.

Advocating for healthy relationships in all ramifications of human interactions.
